Policies & Disclosures

Transparency matters. Here's how things work at The Curated Corner.

Last updated: June 2026

Contact Information

The Curated Corner is an independent blog run by Pallavi K. The best ways to reach us:

  • Email: see our contact page
  • Contact form: thecuratedcorner / contact
  • Pinterest: @thecuratedcorner

We aim to respond to all messages within 2–3 business days.

Return & Refund Policy

The Curated Corner is a content and inspiration blog. We do not sell physical or digital products directly through this website, and we do not process payments or ship orders.

Some posts contain affiliate links to products sold by third-party retailers (such as Amazon and other stores). When you purchase through these links:

  • Your order, payment, shipping, returns, and refunds are handled entirely by that retailer.
  • Returns and refunds are governed by that retailer's own return policy, shown at checkout on their website.
  • We recommend reviewing the retailer's return window and conditions before buying.

If you ever have an issue with a product you bought through one of our links, please contact the retailer directly — and feel free to let us know too so we can keep our recommendations trustworthy.

Affiliate Disclosure

The Curated Corner is a participant in affiliate programs. This means we may earn a small commission, at no extra cost to you, when you click an affiliate link and make a purchase. We only recommend products we genuinely like, and affiliate links are clearly marked as such (e.g. "Affiliate Pick"). Your trust always comes first.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and collect only what we need:

  • Newsletter: if you subscribe, we store your name (optional) and email to send you updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Contact form: if you message us, we store your name, email, and message so we can reply.
  • This information is stored securely and is never sold or shared with third parties.
  • We do not run intrusive tracking. Affiliate retailers and embedded services (e.g. Pinterest) may set their own cookies under their own privacy policies.

To request deletion of your data, just contact us.

Disclaimer

All content is provided for inspiration and informational purposes. Product availability, prices, and trends change over time. We do our best to keep things accurate but can't guarantee it. Always use your own judgement when making purchases.
