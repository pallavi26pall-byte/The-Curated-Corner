10 Summer Outfit Formulas That Always Look Chic

Pallavi K.
Fashion & Style Editor
📅 June 15, 2026 ⏱ 5 min read 📌 1,700 saves

Saving these outfits for later? Pin this to your Style board on Pinterest! 👗

Summer dressing can feel deceptively easy — until you're standing in your wardrobe at 9am, sweating already, with absolutely nothing to wear. The secret to effortless summer style isn't a bigger wardrobe. It's knowing your formulas.

An outfit formula is a reliable combination of pieces that always works. Think of it as a recipe: once you know it, you can swap ingredients and still produce something delicious every time. Here are 10 that never fail.

Formula 1: The Linen Set

A matching linen co-ord — wide-leg trousers and a relaxed button-up — is the chicest lazy outfit in existence. Wear it with sandals and a woven bag. Add gold hoops. Done. This outfit works for brunch, a city day, a beach walk, or even a casual dinner.

Formula 2: Mini Dress + Sneakers

A floaty mini dress instantly becomes cool and casual the moment you swap heels for white sneakers. This is your coffee run, market visit, gallery trip formula. Bonus points for a mini bag and a thin gold necklace.

Formula 3: Straight-Leg Jeans + Crop Top + Mules

The classic that never goes out of style. Choose a slightly cropped tee or a knotted button-up, tuck it into high-waisted straight-leg jeans, and finish with leather mules. It's giving French girl, effortlessly.

"Style is knowing who you are, what you want to say, and not giving a damn." — Orson Welles

Formula 4: Slip Dress + Denim Jacket

A silky slip dress (midi or maxi length) layered under a slightly oversized denim jacket is the perfect transitional outfit. It works from day to night — remove the jacket for dinner, throw it back on for the walk home.

Formula 5: Linen Shorts + Breezy Blouse

High-waisted linen shorts with a slightly billowy, tucked-in blouse is the most wearable summer formula. It's comfortable, flattering, and looks put together even at 30°C. Slide on some espadrilles and you're golden.

Formulas 6–10: The Quick List

  • 6. Maxi skirt + fitted tank + sandals — the easiest elegant look of summer
  • 7. Knit set (bralette + shorts) + oversized blazer — city cool meets beachside ease
  • 8. White dress + coloured bag + metallic sandals — let the accessories do the talking
  • 9. Cargo trousers + crop tee + dad sandals — the street style formula everyone's wearing
  • 10. Printed midi skirt + simple white tee — let one statement piece be the star, keep the rest minimal

The 5 Summer Wardrobe Essentials

Every one of these formulas becomes infinitely easier when you have these five pieces in your wardrobe:

  • White linen wide-leg trousers
  • A silk or satin slip dress in a neutral tone
  • High-waisted straight-leg jeans
  • A crisp white linen shirt (slightly oversized)
  • A simple fitted white tee (worth spending on quality here)
