Canada Day Nail Inspo: 10 Red & White Looks

Pallavi K.
Nail & Beauty Editor
📅 June 29, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read 📌 New

Love these Canada Day looks? Save this pin to your Nail Inspo board on Pinterest! 🍁

July 1st calls for a little red-and-white celebration — right down to your fingertips. Whether you're heading to a backyard barbecue, watching the fireworks, or just want a festive mani, these Canada Day nail ideas range from subtle and chic to full-on glittery patriotic.

Here are 10 looks from the design above, broken down so you can recreate your favourite at home.

🍁 The 10 Canada Day Nail Looks

1. Red Glitter with White Maple Leaf

A deep red glitter base with a single hand-painted white maple leaf as the statement nail. Bold, sparkly, and unmistakably Canadian.

2. Soft Pink with Red Glitter Cuff

A sheer nude-pink base finished with a red glitter half-moon at the base and a tiny red maple leaf. Perfect if you want festive but understated.

3. Creamy White with a Bold Red Leaf

A milky white base with a crisp red maple leaf and a delicate gold stem. The most classic Canada Day nail of the set.

4. Confetti & Stars Accent

A clear-pink base scattered with white stars, gold flakes, and mini red maple leaves — like edible confetti for your nails.

5. Classic Solid Red

Sometimes one rich, glossy red is all you need. A timeless base shade that anchors the whole set.

🎨 Pinterest TipMix and match — pick two or three of these designs across your ten nails rather than repeating one. A solid red, a white maple-leaf accent, and a glittery feature nail is the easiest "designer" combo.

6. White with Scattered Maple Leaves

A clean white base sprinkled with small red maple leaves and tiny red dots. Playful and easy with nail decals.

7. Red & White Candy Swirl

Swirling red and white stripes with fine gold lines and a little gold heart accent. The most artistic look — best done with a thin striping brush.

8. Solid Red Glitter

An all-over red glitter nail that catches the light beautifully under the fireworks. Pair it with the white maple-leaf nail for contrast.

9. Nude with Red Half-Moon

A barely-there nude base with a bold red half-moon at the cuticle and a small red leaf. Modern, minimal, and very Pinterest.

10. Colour-Block with Gold Line

Half white, half red, divided by a thin gold line, finished with a red maple leaf. Clean lines for a high-end finish.

💅 How to Get the Look at Home

You don't need a salon for these — a basic gel kit, a UV/LED lamp, and a few maple-leaf decals will get you there. Here's the simple order:

  • Prep & primer — buff lightly, then apply a bonding primer for grip.
  • Base coat — one thin layer, cure under the lamp.
  • Colour — 1–2 thin coats of your red, white, or nude shade, curing between each.
  • Art — add maple-leaf decals, glitter, or hand-painted details.
  • Top coat — a no-wipe top coat for a glossy, long-lasting finish.
