I'm Pallavi — Welcome to My Curated Corner

The Curated Corner is a space I created for women who find joy in the details — a perfectly painted nail, a beautifully arranged bookshelf, an outfit that makes you feel entirely yourself.

I'm obsessed with Pinterest (always have been), aesthetics, slow living, and sharing things that genuinely inspire me. This blog is my little corner of the internet where I get to do exactly that.

My Philosophy

Beauty in the Everyday

I believe that living beautifully isn't about luxury or perfection. It's about the tiny choices you make every day — choosing the pretty mug, lighting the candle, taking care of your nails, making your bed with intention.

This blog exists to give you ideas, inspo, and the occasional gentle nudge to invest in the small details that make your everyday life feel more curated.

"The details are not the details. They make the design." — Charles Eames
